Project overview

To develop a Android mobile app and website for birdwatchers to record their sightings, add photos and sounds, and view maps of their observations.

    The project includes the development of the following:

  • Mobile app for Android devices
  • Website with a forum for users to share information and ask question
  • Database of bird species
  • Identification guide
  • Map of bird sightings

The app and website have been well-received by users and have helped to improve the way birdwatchers record and share their sightings.

Customer objective

To provide birdwatchers with a mobile app and website that allows them to record their sightings, add photos and sounds, and view maps of their observations in a way that is easy to use, accurate, and reliable.

Business objective

  • Increasing the average time spent by users on the app and website.
  • Increasing the number of in-app purchases made by users.
  • Generating positive reviews from users.
  • Building a strong brand reputation for the GibbonMM app and website.

Gibbon Multimedia

Gibbon Multimedia is a software company based in Peregian Springs, Australia. The company was founded in 1998 by two brothers, who are passionate about nature and technology. Gibbon Multimedia develops software for a variety of purposes, including birdwatching, education, and conservation.

One of the most popular products developed by Gibbon Multimedia is the GibbonMM app, which is a mobile app for birdwatchers. The app allows users to record their sightings, add photos and sounds, and view maps of their observations. The app also has a built-in identification guide and a forum where users can share information and ask questions.
